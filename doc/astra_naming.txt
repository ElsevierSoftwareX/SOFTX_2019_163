Concepts
========

- *Volume geometry*, the area in which the scanned object resides
- *Projection geometry*, the geometry of the rays (often in angles/offset)
- *Projector* is the object that constructs an implicit matrix from a projection geometry, requires a weighing kernel (line/strip/Joseph)
